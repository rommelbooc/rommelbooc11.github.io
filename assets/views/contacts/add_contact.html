CLASS SELECTION GUIDE

Here is the rough guide for selecting an Occupation Class. Note that, because of variations between, insurers, sometimes the only wat to know for sure is to call an underwriter.

OCCUPATION CLASS 1

Professionals (usually tertiary qualified) typically earning over $80,000 pa.

OCCUPATION CLASS 2

Office workers who do not perform any manual labour

OCCUPATION CLASS 3

Skilled trades people (for example a qualified builder) or none-desk based workers (for example technician, shopkeeper etc).

OCCUPATION CLASS 4

Skilled or semi-skilled manual workers and heavy manchinery operators who are not exposed to health hazards.

OCCUPATION CLASS 5

Housewife / Househusband / Student
